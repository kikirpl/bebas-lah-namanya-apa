PROFILE

Astolfo Kun

Fate Grand Order

Astolfo is a former Servant turned into a human by his master's wish to the Holy Grail. He is the former Rider of Black from the Great Holy Grail War thirteen years before. Kept manifested by the Greater Grail, he is recruited by the Mages' Association and willingly makes a Master-Servant contract with Leo Solaris, if not a temporary one.
